Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about reservations, amenities, and hotel policies.

Do you have family rooms?

Yes, we offer spacious family rooms designed for comfort and privacy. These rooms are ideal for families and groups. Extra bedding can be arranged if needed.

Do you have free Wi-Fi at the hotel?

Yes, free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. You can stay connected in your room and all public areas. No extra charges are required.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in time is at 5:00 AM and check-out time is at 5:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are available upon request and subject to availability. Additional charges may apply.

Do you have accessible rooms for guests with disabilities?

Yes, we have ADA-compliant accessible rooms featuring wider doorways, roll-in showers, lowered fixtures, and visual alarms. Please mention your accessibility requirements when making your reservation.
