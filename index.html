Welcome to Humming Birds Suite!

Located in the heart of Jaffna, our hotel combines comfort, style, and relaxation. With well-furnished rooms, a calm atmosphere, and essentials like free high-speed WiFi, 24-hour check-in, and dedicated guest support, we ensure a smooth, hassle-free stay.

Whether you’re visiting Jaffna for business, family, or leisure, our hotel delivers a memorable stay through premium service and warm hospitality. We focus on cleanliness, comfort, and personalized care to ensure you feel welcome and at home throughout your visit.
